Why should you choose fabulous coffee?


We are the most complete Single Origin Supplier in Indonesia, with authentic and the best quality Arabica and Robusta coffee beans from Sabang to Merauke. Such as Takengon, Bajawa, Kintamani, Ciwidey, Sunda Jahe, Nagari Lasi, Blue Batak, Lencoh Merapi, Java Tobacco, Papua, Lampung Robusta, Dampit Malang Robusta, Flores Robusta, Java Robusta and many more.


Indonesian coffee is very well known throughout the world for the quality and the best taste.


What really determines the quality of coffee:

60% Raw Green Beans + 30% Roasting + 10% Brewing



The Best Quality

Providing coffee variants from all over Indonesia with the best quality, highest level and processed with international standards.

AICE Certification

Standardization

The roasting process is of the highest quality, through a variety of methods with international standard qualifications
