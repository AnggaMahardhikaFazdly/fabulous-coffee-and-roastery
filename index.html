Why should you choose fabulous coffee?


We are the most complete Single Origin Supplier in Indonesia, with authentic and the best quality Arabica and Robusta coffee beans from Sabang to Merauke. Such as Takengon, Bajawa, Kintamani, Ciwidey, Sunda Jahe, Nagari Lasi, Blue Batak, Lencoh Merapi, Java Tobacco, Papua, Lampung Robusta, Dampit Malang Robusta, Flores Robusta, Java Robusta and many more.
